# Code of Conduct

Awesome Sentrix is a curated resource list for Sentrix Chain developers, validators, users, and ecosystem contributors.

Please keep all discussions respectful, technical, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Keep discussions relevant to Sentrix Chain and this repository.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Use clear and factual language.
- Help maintain a useful resource list for the community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not welcome:

- harassment or personal attacks
- spam or scam links
- impersonation
- abusive or discriminatory language
- misleading or purely promotional submissions
- malicious links, phishing, malware, or unsafe resources
- off-topic arguments unrelated to Sentrix Chain

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove issues, pull requests, comments, or resources that are abusive, misleading, malicious, spammy, or unrelated to this repository.

Repeated or serious violations may result in being blocked from participating in the repository.
